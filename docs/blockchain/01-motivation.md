# Motivation

## Bridging Programmable and Non-Programmable Blockchains

The decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem has undergone a remarkable evolution, transitioning from experimental projects to robust solutions that address the challenge of permissionless financial freedom. This transformation has been nothing short of revolutionary, as DeFi protocols have matured to offer sophisticated financial instruments and services.

The security and integrity of these DeFi protocols are fundamentally tied to the underlying blockchain infrastructure they operate on. This interdependence highlights the critical role that blockchains play in ensuring the safety and efficiency of decentralized financial services. As a result, the choice of blockchain becomes a pivotal decision for any DeFi protocol aiming to provide secure and reliable services.

However, not all blockchains are created equal. While programmable blockchains, such as Ethereum, offer smart contract capabilities that enable complex DeFi applications, there exists a significant number of non-programmable blockchains that lack these essential features. These blockchains, often referred to as "legacy" or "non-Turing-complete" blockchains, are inherently isolated from the vibrant and rapidly evolving DeFi infrastructure.

## The Challenge of Non-Programmable Blockchains

Non-programmable blockchains present a unique challenge in the context of DeFi. Their inability to support smart contracts means they cannot directly participate in the decentralized financial ecosystem. This isolation creates a significant barrier to achieving true decentralization and security within the broader DeFi landscape.

Currently, there are solutions available that attempt to bridge this gap. These solutions often rely on centralized services, which introduce single points of failure and undermine the core principles of decentralization. Additionally, some solutions offer only weak guarantees, failing to provide the robust security assurances required for trustless financial transactions. This lack of strong guarantees can lead to increased risks and potential vulnerabilities in the DeFi ecosystem.

## Motivation for an Intermediate Layer-1 Blockchain

The primary motivation behind the Tangent Cash is to prioritize decentralization and security above all else. To enable DeFi to function securely across both programmable and non-programmable blockchains, we propose the introduction of an intermediate layer-1 blockchain. This innovative approach acts as a glue that connects different blockchains, eliminating the need for direct interaction with peer-to-peer (P2P) services.

By serving as an intermediary, this layer-1 blockchain provides stronger guarantees and reduces manual intervention, thereby enhancing the overall security and efficiency of cross-chain DeFi operations. This intermediate layer ensures that non-programmable blockchains can participate in the DeFi ecosystem without compromising on decentralization or security. It effectively creates a bridge that allows these blockchains to tap into the vast potential of DeFi applications.

## Smart Contract Execution Layer

While DeFi smart contracts running on programmable blockchains are robust and versatile, the diversity of approaches across different blockchains presents a significant challenge. Each blockchain may have its own unique set of smart contract standards, execution environments, and security models, leading to potential incompatibilities and increased complexity.

To address this issue, Tangent Cash introduces a dedicated smart contract execution layer. This layer is designed to be far less error-prone and more reliable than direct cross-chain contract interactions. By providing a standardized environment for smart contract execution, it ensures interoperability and consistency across the DeFi ecosystem, regardless of the underlying blockchain.

This standardization helps in reducing the complexity associated with cross-chain operations and enhances the reliability of smart contracts executed across different blockchains. It creates a unified framework that simplifies the process of developing and deploying DeFi applications, making it easier for developers to create innovative financial solutions.

## TL;DR

The Tangent Cash aims to bridge the gap between programmable and non-programmable blockchains, enabling true decentralization and security in the DeFi ecosystem. Through the introduction of an intermediate layer-1 blockchain and a dedicated smart contract execution layer, we can overcome the challenges posed by non-programmable blockchains and create a more inclusive and secure DeFi landscape.

By prioritizing decentralization and security, Tangent Cash paves the way for a future where financial freedom is truly permissionless and accessible to all. This vision of an inclusive and secure DeFi ecosystem benefits not only the existing users but also attracts new participants who were previously excluded due to the limitations of non-programmable blockchains.

In essence, Tangent Cash is a step towards a more interconnected and resilient DeFi future, where the barriers between different blockchain technologies are broken down, and true financial freedom can be achieved.
