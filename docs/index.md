# Welcome

Tangent Cash is a decentralized cross-chain smart contract execution layer and this documentation site provides comprehensive guides and references regarding this protocol.

## Getting Started

Ready to dive in?

1. **Developers**: Start with the [Wallet App Guide](app/01-overview.md) or explore our [Blockchain Documentation](blockchain/01-motivation.md).
2. **Node Operators**: Head straight to the [Node Operations Guide](node/01-install.md).
3. **Users**: Download and set up the [Wallet App](app/01-overview.md) to begin your journey with Tangent Cash.

We're excited to have you join us in shaping the future of decentralized technology! If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our community support.
